Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Wings Junior School.

What are the admission requirements?

We require a completed application form, birth certificate, previous school records (if applicable), and a parent/guardian interview. Please visit our admission page for complete details.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable door-to-door transportation services for our students. Our vehicles are well-maintained and driven by experienced drivers.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain small class sizes with a maximum of 25 students per class and three teachers per section to ensure personalized attention for every child.

Can I schedule a school visit?

Absolutely! We welcome parents to visit our school. Please contact us to schedule a convenient time for a tour of our facilities and to meet our staff.
